## Viral Content Patterns

### High-Engagement Hooks Working in 2026

1. **Contrarian statements**: "Unpopular opinion: [widely accepted belief] is terrible"
2. **Data surprises**: Counterintuitive statistics that challenge assumptions
3. **Transformation stories**: Before/after with specific outcomes
4. **Framework carousels**: Complete systems in visual format
5. **Bold predictions**: Industry forecasts with conviction
6. **Problem diagnosis**: Deep dive into root causes others miss
7. **Anti-pattern content**: What NOT to do (taboo but valuable)
8. **Community questions**: Spark genuine conversation

## Cross-Platform Content Repurposing

### Efficient Workflow
```
Long-form LinkedIn article → Twitter thread (5-10 tweets)
                    ↓
            PDF carousel (key points)
                    ↓
            Short video (60-90s)
                    ↓
            Instagram carousel + Reels
```

### Key Principles
- Adapt format to platform, not copy-paste
- LinkedIn rewards completeness; Twitter rewards brevity
- Video原生 to each platform
- No external links in original posts; add in comments
