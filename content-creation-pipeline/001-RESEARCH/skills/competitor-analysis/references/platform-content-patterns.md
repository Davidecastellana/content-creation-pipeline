# Platform Content Patterns

## LinkedIn Content Patterns

### Top-Performing Content Types
1. **Document posts** (PDF carousel): 2-3x more reach than standard posts
2. **Thought leadership articles**: High engagement from professionals
3. **Personal stories with insights**: Authenticity wins
4. **Data-driven posts**: Stats and research citations

### Engagement Patterns
- Average CTR on links: 2-4%
- Average engagement rate: 2-5% for organic
- Comments > Shares > Reactions (algorithm priority)
- Saves indicate high-value content

### Posting Cadence
- 3-5 posts per week optimal
- Consistent posting builds algorithm favor
- Best performing days: Tue-Thu

---

## Twitter/X Content Patterns

### Top-Performing Content Types
1. **Thread deep dives**: High engagement and shares
2. **Hot takes/contrarian opinions**: Viral potential
3. **Quick tips**: Value in brevity
4. **News commentary**: Timely and relevant

### Engagement Patterns
- Average engagement rate: 0.5-1%
- Early engagement critical (first 30 min)
- Video links perform well
- Reply chains increase visibility

### Posting Cadence
- 5-10 tweets per day
- Space out posting
- Best performing days: Tue-Thu

---

## Instagram Content Patterns

### Top-Performing Content Types
1. **Reels**: Highest reach potential
2. **Carousels**: High save and share rates
3. **User-generated content**: Trust and authenticity
4. **Behind-the-scenes**: Personal connection

### Engagement Patterns
- Average engagement rate: 1-3%
- Save rate most important algorithm signal
- Reels reach beyond followers
- Stories maintain daily connection

### Posting Cadence
- 3-5 feed posts per week
- Daily stories for connection
- 2-3 reels per week minimum

---

## Cross-Platform Content Themes

### Evergreen Winners
- "How to" educational content
- Industry insights and trends
- Behind-the-scenes authenticity
- User testimonials and case studies

### High-Engagement Hooks
- Questions that spark comments
- Bold statements that invite debate
- Data points that surprise
- Stories of transformation
