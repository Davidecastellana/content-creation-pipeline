# Trend Research

## Process

1. **Define scope**: industry, target audience, platforms
2. **Search trends**: use web search and social listening for trending topics
3. **Read references**: load `content-creation-pipeline/001-RESEARCH/skills/trend-research/references/trend-methodology.md`
4. **Analyze patterns**: identify content formats and themes driving engagement
5. **Validate trends**: cross-reference with platform-native trend tools
6. **Generate report**: produce trend brief with content recommendations

## What to Research

### Platform Trends
- LinkedIn: trending articles, popular discussions, industry news
- Twitter/X: trending hashtags, viral threads, news cycles
- Instagram: trending audio, Reels topics, aesthetic trends

### Content Format Trends
- What formats are gaining traction?
- Video length trends (short-form vs long-form)
- Carousel vs single image performance patterns
- UGC vs branded content reception

### Topic Trends
- Industry-specific news and developments
- Pain points and conversations gaining momentum
- Seasonal/cyclical content opportunities
- Cultural moments relevant to audience

### Engagement Signals
- Which topics drive comments vs shares vs saves?
- Optimal posting times by platform
- Hashtag performance patterns

## Trend Validation

Before acting on any trend:
- Cross-reference with 3+ independent sources
- Verify trend has momentum (not just one viral post)
- Assess brand fit before recommending
- Check trend longevity (fad vs lasting)

## Quality Gates

- Never recommend exploiting tragedies, crises, or sensitive topics
- Verify trend data is current (within 7 days)
- Always assess brand alignment before recommending
- Minimum 2 sources per identified trend

## Output

### Deliverables
- `TREND-REPORT.md`: Trending topics and content opportunities
- Hashtag recommendations by platform
- Content angle opportunities
- Timing recommendations
