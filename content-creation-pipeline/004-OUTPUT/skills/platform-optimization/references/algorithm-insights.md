# Algorithm Insights by Platform

## LinkedIn Algorithm

### Priority Signals
1. **Early engagement** (first 30 min): Comments > Shares > Reactions
2. **Connection depth**: Comments from 1st-degree connections rank higher
3. **Save rate**: Saves signal high-value content
4. **Conversation**: Comments with replies boost reach

### Optimization Tactics
- Ask questions to spark comments
- Write controversial-but-professional takes
- Add "Tag someone who..." CTA
- Time posts for 8am-10am Tue-Thu
- Engage within first 30 min to boost signal

### What Reduces Reach
- Excessive links (reduces reach significantly)
- Posting too frequently (>2/day)
- Low engagement in first hour
- Avoided topics flagged by LinkedIn

## Twitter/X Algorithm

### Priority Signals
1. **Early engagement**: First 30 minutes critical
2. **Follower ratio**: High following/follower ratio gets boosts
3. **Media inclusion**: Tweets with images/video perform better
4. **Conversation signals**: Replies boost algorithmic reach

### Optimization Tactics
- Add 1-2 relevant hashtags
- Include media (image/video) in every tweet
- Tag relevant accounts
- Engage with replies in first hour
- Time posts for 8am-10am, 12pm-1pm Tue-Thu

### What Reduces Reach
- Excessive hashtags (>2)
- Low engagement in first 30 min
- Tweeting rapidly in short bursts
- Engagement bait (fake interactions)

## Instagram Algorithm

### Priority Signals
1. **Save rate**: Most important signal
2. **Share rate**: Second most important
3. **Engagement rate**: Likes + Comments / Reach
4. **Follower recency**: Recent follows signal interest

### Optimization Tactics
- End captions with "Save this if..." or "Tag someone who..."
- Add 3-5 relevant hashtags (not excessive)
- Use first comment for additional hashtags
- Time for 11am-1pm, 7pm-9pm Mon-Thu
- Use 1-2 strategic emojis (not excessive)

### What Reduces Reach
- Excessive hashtags (>30 in comments)
- Posting too frequently
- Low save/share rates
- Engagement bait violations
