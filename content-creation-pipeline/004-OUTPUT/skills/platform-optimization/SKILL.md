# Platform Optimization

## Process

1. **Read formatted content**: get platform-formatted copy
2. **Analyze platform algorithms**: each platform favors different signals
3. **Apply optimizations**: add hashtags, emojis, timing, format tweaks
4. **Read references**: load `content-creation-pipeline/004-OUTPUT/skills/platform-optimization/references/algorithm-insights.md`
5. **Validate against algorithm**: ensure content matches platform signals
6. **Generate publishing specs**: timing, tagging, and format recommendations
7. **Output optimized content**: ready for immediate publishing

## Algorithm Optimization

### LinkedIn Algorithm
The LinkedIn algorithm prioritizes:
1. **Early engagement** (first 30 min): Comments > Shares > Reactions
2. **Connection depth**: Comments from 1st-degree connections rank higher
3. **Save rate**: Saves signal high-value content
4. **Conversation**: Comments with replies boost reach

**Optimization tactics:**
- Ask questions to spark comments
- Write controversial-but-professional takes
- Add "Tag someone who..." CTA
- Time posts for 8am-10am Tue-Thu
- Engage within first 30 min to boost signal

### Twitter/X Algorithm
Twitter prioritizes:
1. **Early engagement**: First 30 minutes critical
2. **Follower ratio**: Accounts with high following/follower ratio get boosts
3. **Media inclusion**: Tweets with images/video perform better
4. **Conversation signals**: Replies boost algorithmic reach

**Optimization tactics:**
- Add 1-2 relevant hashtags
- Include media (image/video) in every tweet
- Tag relevant accounts
- Engage with replies in first hour
- Time posts for 8am-10am, 12pm-1pm Tue-Thu

### Instagram Algorithm
Instagram prioritizes:
1. **Save rate**: Most important signal
2. **Share rate**: Second most important
3. **Engagement rate**: Likes + Comments / Reach
4. **Follower recency**: Recent follows signal interest

**Optimization tactics:**
- End captions with "Save this if..." or "Tag someone who..."
- Add 3-5 relevant hashtags (not excessive)
- Use first comment for additional hashtags
- Time for 11am-1pm, 7pm-9pm Mon-Thu
- Use 1-2 strategic emojis (not excessive)

## Hashtag Strategy

### LinkedIn Hashtags
- **Quantity**: 3-5 relevant hashtags
- **Placement**: End of post OR first comment
- **Selection**: Mix of broad (#marketing) + niche (#B2B SaaS)
- **Research**: Use LinkedIn's suggested hashtags

### Twitter/X Hashtags
- **Quantity**: 1-2 relevant hashtags
- **Placement**: Embedded mid-tweet OR at end
- **Selection**: Trending + evergreen mix
- **Research**: Check trending hashtags before posting

### Instagram Hashtags
- **Quantity**: 3-5 in caption + up to 30 in first comment
- **Placement**: Caption end OR first comment
- **Selection**: Mix of popular + niche + branded
- **Strategy**: Rotate hashtag sets to avoid shadowban

## Timing Recommendations

### Optimal Posting Times
| Platform | Best Days | Best Times |
|----------|----------|------------|
| LinkedIn | Tue-Thu | 8am-10am, 5pm-6pm local |
| Twitter/X | Tue-Thu | 8am-10am, 12pm-1pm local |
| Instagram | Mon-Thu | 11am-1pm, 7pm-9pm local |

### Time Zone Handling
- Always use audience's time zone
- US audience: EST/PT for posts
- Global audience: Stagger by major time zones

## Tagging Strategy

### LinkedIn
- Tag relevant companies/persons in post
- Tag employees who engaged with similar content
- Use company page for branded content

### Twitter/X
- Tag accounts mentioned in content
- Tag relevant influencers
- Avoid excessive tagging (max 3)

### Instagram
- Tag brand partners
- Tag content creators if UGC
- Use location tag for local content

## Emoji Strategy

### When to Use Emojis
- **LinkedIn**: Sparse, professional (1-2 max)
- **Twitter/X**: Strategic accent (1-2 max)
- **Instagram**: Moderate, visual appeal (3-5 max)

### Emoji Rules
- Never replace words with emojis
- Use emojis as visual anchors, not decoration
- Match emoji tone to brand voice
- Avoid overused emoji combinations
