# Brief Collection

## Process

1. **Introduction**: explain brief collection purpose and expected outcomes
2. **Business overview**: product/service, unique value proposition
3. **Goals**: what success looks like (awareness, engagement, leads, sales)
4. **Audience**: target demographics, pain points, content preferences
5. **Key messages**: core themes and talking points to communicate
6. **Tone of voice**: brand personality calibration
7. **Platform priorities**: where content will be published
8. **Success metrics**: how success will be measured
9. **Constraints**: deadlines, approval processes, content restrictions

## Brief Sections

### 1. Business Overview
- What does the business do?
- What makes it unique?
- What is the primary offer?

### 2. Campaign Goals
- **Primary objective**: Awareness | Engagement | Lead Generation | Sales
- **Secondary objective**: (if applicable)
- **Timeline**: Campaign duration and key milestones
- **Success metric**: What defines campaign success?

### 3. Target Audience
- **Demographics**: role, industry, company size, location
- **Psychographics**: values, challenges, content preferences
- **Pain points**: what problems does the audience face?
- **Content appetite**: how do they prefer to consume content?

### 4. Key Messages
List 3-5 core messages to communicate:
1. [Primary message]
2. [Secondary message]
3. ...

### 5. Tone of Voice
Calibrate on these axes:
- **Formal ↔ Casual**: 1-10 scale
- **Bold ↔ Subtle**: 1-10 scale
- **Rational ↔ Emotional**: 1-10 scale
- **Descriptive words**: [adjectives to match]

### 6. Platform Priorities
Rank by importance:
1. [Platform] - [why]
2. [Platform] - [why]
3. [Platform] - [why]

### 7. Success Metrics
- Engagement targets: likes, comments, shares, saves
- Reach targets: impressions, unique reach
- Lead/sales targets: [specific numbers]
- Brand metrics: sentiment, awareness lift

### 8. Content Themes
Topics to cover in content:
1. [Theme]
2. [Theme]
3. [Theme]

### 9. Constraints
- Approval workflow: who needs to approve?
- Deadlines: content calendar dates
- Content restrictions: what's off-limits?
- Brand assets: logos, colors, imagery guidelines

## Quality Gates

- Every brief MUST have a clear primary objective
- Every brief MUST identify target audience
- Every brief MUST specify at least 3 key messages
- Briefs without success metrics are incomplete

## Output

### Deliverables
- `CONTENT-BRIEF.md`: Complete collected brief
- `AUDIENCE-PROFILE.md`: Detailed audience description
- `MESSAGE-MAP.md`: Key messages mapped to audience pain points
