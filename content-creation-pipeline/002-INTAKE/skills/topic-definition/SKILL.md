# Topic Definition

## Process

1. **Review inputs**: read competitor analysis, trend report, and content brief
2. **Identify intersections**: find where competitor gaps + trends + brief messages overlap
3. **Generate topic candidates**: create 10-15 potential content angles
4. **Score topics**: rate by differentiation, trend relevance, brand fit, engagement potential
5. **Select topics**: choose 5-8 topics for content calendar
6. **Define angles**: for each topic, specify the unique angle and hook

## Input Sources

### From Research Stage
- `COMPETITOR-ANALYSIS.md`: Content gaps and differentiation opportunities
- `TREND-REPORT.md`: Trending topics and content opportunities

### From Intake Stage
- `CONTENT-BRIEF.md`: Goals, messages, audience profile
- `AUDIENCE-PROFILE.md`: Pain points and content preferences

## Topic Generation Framework

### Content Pillars (Start Here)
Create 3-5 content pillars that align with brand and audience:
- **Educational**: teach something valuable
- **Entertaining**: humor, stories, behind-the-scenes
- **Promotional**: product/service highlights, offers
- **Engagement**: questions, polls, discussions
- **Social proof**: testimonials, case studies, reviews

### Topic Scoring Matrix

| Criterion | Weight | Score (1-5) |
|-----------|--------|-------------|
| Differentiation | 25% | Is this a competitor gap? |
| Trend relevance | 20% | Is this currently resonating? |
| Brand fit | 20% | Does this align with brand? |
| Audience interest | 20% | Will audience care? |
| Engagement potential | 15% | Does this invite interaction? |

### Topic Selection Criteria
- Must score ≥3.5 on at least 4 of 5 criteria
- Must address at least 1 key message from brief
- Must fit within tone of voice guidelines

## Content Angles

For each selected topic, define:

### The Hook
First 3 words that capture attention:
- Pain point: "Stop wasting time..."
- Question: "Did you know that..."
- Statement: "The truth about..."
- Statistic: "87% of marketers..."

### The Angle
The specific perspective or framing:
- Before/after transformation
- Myth-busting
- Counter-intuitive insight
- Behind-the-scenes reveal
- Expert interview format
- Case study narrative

### The Takeaway
What the reader learns or feels:
- A new perspective
- A actionable tip
- A emotional connection
- A sense of belonging

## Platform Adaptation

### LinkedIn Topics
- Professional value and insights
- Industry thought leadership
- Career and productivity tips

### Twitter/X Topics
- Quick tips and hot takes
- Industry news commentary
- Thread-worthy deep dives

### Instagram Topics

- Community spotlight
